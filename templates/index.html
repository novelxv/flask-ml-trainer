  1. Upload Dataset: Click "Upload Dataset" and select your Excel or CSV file.
  2. Preview Data: Review your data and select the target column for prediction.
  3. Train Model: Configure training parameters and click "Train Model".
  4. Make Predictions: Go to the Predict page and input values to get classifications.
  5. View Results: See prediction results with confidence scores and probabilities.
